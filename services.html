    FAQs

    Are you insured?

    Yes I am fully insured so rest assured your pups are in the best hands

    How are my dogs transported?

    For group walks the dogs are transported by car with dog seat belts as I know lots of dogs don't like crates. My dogs are VIPs (very important pooches)

    Do you work with reactive dogs?

    I work with all dogs if me, the dog and the owner all feel the fit is right

    Do dogs get off lead?

    Only with owners permission and if I am happy with their recall, on-lead walks can be accommodated
